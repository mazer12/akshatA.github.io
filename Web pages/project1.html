    • STOLEN PAIR

    • Platform : Windows
    • Engine: Unity
    • Language : C#
    • Duration : 2.5 months
    • Project type : Group project
    • Team Size : 4 (2 Artists, 1 Designer and me)
    • Role : Lead game programmer, sounds, UI elements
    Overwiew :

    This is a two-player FPS game. The devil has stolen a sock (yes a sock) from the god. God sends a pair of angel siblings to retrieve the sock and exterminate this devil. The angel who wins this battle will receive their wings from the god. Each of the two players will play as one of the angels, and the angel with the most damage dealt to the devil will win the game.

    Gameplay video :
    Gameplay Mechanics:
    Gliding:

    Players can hold 'v' to glide while jumping.




    Weapon swap:

    Players can swap between two weapons, a pistol and a rifle. The rifle deals more damage but has a low fire rate and the pistol deals less damage but can shoot faster.




    Enemy:

    The enemy AI is made using a finite state machine. It generates a random path for the enemy to patrol in every loop. This means that the enemy will move a different path after patrolling a path once.




    Networking:

    PUN networking solution is used for the multiplayer aspect of the game.
