    • Magnus Effect in Tennis

    • Platform : Windows
    • Made in: Unity
    • Language : C#
    • Duration : 2 months
    • Project type : Master's project
    Overwiew :

    This was created for my master's research project at Abertay University. It was focused on developing an algorithm to simulate the trajectory of a ball under the effect of the Magnus force. The Magnus force in sports refers to the aerodynamic force that affects a spinning object's trajectory, causing it to curve or swerve due to differences in air pressure around the object. Most of the Indie games I came across either use approximations for the Magnus effect or skip this force altogether. I built this algorithm around tennis to test and compare the trajectories of the ball and the efficiency of the algorithm. The research was done with the goal of understanding if a separate complicated algorithm for the Magnus force can provide better results than the approximations without losing any efficiency, and how to implement them. Read more about the results of my Dissertation.
